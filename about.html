About Us

The sudden lockdown during the Corona Pandemic amplified the issues that the senior citizens and their children face due to their hectic schedules. Such problems made Ushmita Dutta, the need to bridge up this gap.
Right now, we are at a very nascent stage and thus we only operate in the city of Jamshedpur, Jharkhand. If luck favours we will be expanding "Aapka Aashirvaad" to many more places as well.

Our Mission

  • Reach out to all the people in need.
  • Serve with morals and ethics.
  • Deliver services on time.

Our Vision

  • In order to be the best in services, accept criticism as well as appreciation.
  • Support those in need like a family.
  • No delay in help.
